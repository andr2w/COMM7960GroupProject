Dataset#

There is only one fake news video detection dataset that contains the social context informaiton, that is FakeSV [1]. The FakeSV dataset contains the fake and real news videos from a popular Chinese short video platforom douyin. The FakeSV dataset consists of 1,827 fake news videos and 1,827 real news video. Evauluation Metrics#

We utilize the following metrics to evaluate the performance in our pre-experiments.

  • Accuracy (Acc.).
  • Precision (Prec.).
  • Recall (Recall).
  • Macro $\mathbf{F}1$-score ($\mathbf{F}1$).

Building on the work of previous studies [1], we conducted preliminary experiments in which we selected a broad array of fake news video detection methods to compare their performance against each other. The fake news video detection methods employied in our pre-experiments are shown in the following:

  • Hou et al. [2] use the textual inforamtion and engagement features such as likes of the comments and train a linear kernel SVM to debunk the fake news videos.
  • Medina et al. [3] use the tf-idf to encode the title and comments into neumerical representation and train a logistic regression to classifiy wether a given video is fake news video or real news video.
  • Choi and Ko. [4] use an adversarial neural networks to distinguish the real and fake news videos.
  • Shang et al. [5] use the extracted speech text to guide the learning of visual object features, use MFCC features to enhance the speech textual features, and then use a co-attention module to fuse the visual and speech information for classification.
  • Qi et al. [1] can be found in the Methodology page.
